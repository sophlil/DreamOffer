Every offer started as a dream.

When applying for internships/jobs, Computer Science students may find themselves sending out hundreds of applications. DreamOffer, a “Job Application Tracking” database driven web application, helps applicants keep track of where they have applied already and when their applications were sent in an organized and simple to use system. DreamOffer stores data for hundreds of applicants from various backgrounds. It also stores data for thousands of different applications for hundreds of companies, and there is still room for growth in these numbers. When Applicants record new Applications with our database, they record which Positions at which Companies they applied for, and who the CompanyRecruiters are for those Positions.
